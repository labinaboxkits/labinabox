Our story

Built by science teachers, for serious science at home.

Lab in a Box was created by UK secondary science teachers with years of classroom and laboratory experience. We understand curriculum requirements, practical technique, laboratory craft and the common pitfalls that can stop students gaining confidence.

We saw a gap between generic STEM activity kits and the rigorous practical work students need for future study and careers in science. Our kits bring proper scientific thinking into the home: purposeful experiments, reliable methods, careful recording and meaningful evaluation.
