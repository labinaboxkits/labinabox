Our story

Built by science teachers, for serious science beyond the lab.

Lab in a Box was created by UK secondary science teachers with years of classroom and laboratory experience. We understand curriculum requirements, practical technique, laboratory craft and the common pitfalls that can stop students gaining confidence.

We saw a gap between generic STEM activity kits and the rigorous practical work students need for future study and careers in science. Our core equipment and modules bring proper scientific thinking into homes, tutoring spaces and schools with limited lab access: purposeful experiments, reliable methods, careful recording and meaningful evaluation.
